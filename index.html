Hot on Coffee & Big on Food

Welcome to Kofi & Co.

Kofi&Co. {pronunciation: Koh - phee} is an independent restaurant serving speciality coffee, wholesome plates, sweet treats and a full bar menu. We passionately believe in supporting our community and local producers and take pride in preparing fresh and creative dishes, using quality ingredients from right here in Yorkshire.We have two floors of inside seating as well as our heated and covered front terrace and our private rear garden.
